About Grace

Rooted in tradition.
Taught with care.

Grace Galligan is a Dublin-based professional musician and music teacher with over a decade of performing and teaching experience across Irish traditional and classical music. Holding an MSc in Music Performance from the University of Limerick — awarded on scholarship — Grace brings both academic rigour and deep musical passion to every lesson.

An All Ireland Champion in Uilleann Pipes and medallist at Fleadh Ceoil na hÉireann in Sean Nós singing, Grace is one of Ireland's finest traditional musicians. She is a current member of the National Folk Orchestra of Ireland, whose performances have been broadcast on RTÉ and TG4, and a former member of RTÉ Cór na nÓg.

Grace has performed as a soloist at the Oireachtas (broadcast live on RTÉ Radio na Gaeltachta), toured with Rhythm of the Dance across the UK and Ireland, and represented Ireland at the Milwaukee Irish Festival — the largest Irish festival in North America — where she performed and taught at the summer school.

Currently teaching at Willow Park (Blackrock College) and across multiple platforms, Grace's teaching spans all ages and levels. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced musician, lessons are built entirely around you.
